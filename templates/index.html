Welcome to Router


Router is a travel destination optimizer.

With Router, you will be able to schedule your trip with minimum cost.

You can click to the button below to try Router.


Destinations

Instructions

  • 1 Select your starting city (Where you are departing from, probably your location)
  • 2 Select your trip's start date date from calender located at right side of the starting city field
  • 3 Enter cities to visit and days you want to spend in those cities
  • 4 If you want you want add more destinations by clicking on "Add new destinations" button
  • 5 Click to calculate button in order to get your results
